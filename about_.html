What is a guest pass?

To see all the personal trainers contact details you need to register and grab a guest pass. This is to ensure the privacy of the personal trainers and reduce spam and over enthusiastic marketers.
You do this with either your Twitter or Facebook account and we have made it easy and safe for you. The one day pass is free and easy to get.
If you are just browsing the directory a guest pass is not required.
