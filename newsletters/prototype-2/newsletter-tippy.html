Tips to help avoid mouth ulcers
 
  • Use Squigle Toothpaste or Tooth Builder Toothpaste as they are SLS free.
  • Avoid irritating flavours such as spearmint and cinnamon. 
  • Try to reduce stress (easier said than done!).
  • Keep fit and healthy and try not to get too run down.
