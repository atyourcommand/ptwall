New York New York — Jonathan Angelilli

Don’t believe the internet marketing trainers who say all you need is a blog and a website to get clients, that is a complete load of bull! Word of mouth marketing works better than anything. Blow your clients away with your services, that is the best way to start your business, they will tell other people. Then when you have money, you can invest in internet technologies.. read more»
  1. Blow your clients away with your services  
  2. Try specializing eg Seniors, aquatics, remedial  
  3. Add to your base certifications always
  4. Develop your interpersonal skills and your unique style 
  5. Work pro-bono and find a experienced trainer to work alongside to start with
