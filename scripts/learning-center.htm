Getting Started

New to Nitro Reader? You've come to the right place.

The Learning Center is designed to give you an overview of the most popular tasks and tools and will get you up and running in just a few minutes.

Use your mouse to explore the interactive UI below and tooltips will appear explaining what each feature does.
